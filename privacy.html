Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

MonetaReady is a free pre-upload video analysis tool designed to help creators maximize monetization opportunities on TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and Instagram Reels.

We respect your privacy. All video processing happens directly in your browser using client-side JavaScript. No videos, audio, or personal data are uploaded to any server or stored by us.

We do not collect names, email addresses, IP addresses for tracking, or any other personal information. We do not use analytics cookies or third-party trackers.

Your uploaded video files are processed temporarily in memory and are automatically cleared when you close or refresh the page.

Data Retention: None. We store nothing.
