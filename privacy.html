Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 04, 2026

MonetaReady is a free pre-upload video analysis tool for short-form content creators.

We respect your privacy. This app processes your video entirely in your browser. No videos, audio, or personal data are uploaded to any server or stored by us.

We do not collect names, emails, IP addresses, or any tracking data.
